Data-Driven Digital Marketing: Applying the Scientific Method

A comprehensive course for mastering data-driven marketing through the scientific method and A/B testing.

Course Benefits

Eliminate Guesswork

Learn how to use data to drive marketing decisions, reducing uncertainty and increasing effectiveness.

Hands-On Experience

Gain practical experience with real-world case studies and a capstone project that applies your learning.

Optimize ROI

Use A/B testing and statistical analysis to maximize return on investment for all marketing channels.

Course Curriculum

Module 1: Introduction to Data-Driven Marketing and the Scientific Method

  • Lesson 1.1: Overview of Data-Driven Marketing
    • Why data-driven approaches outperform intuition-based strategies
    • Key benefits of using data in marketing, such as precision targeting, budget efficiency, and better ROI
  • Lesson 1.2: The Scientific Method in Marketing
    • Defining the scientific method: hypothesis, experimentation, observation, and analysis
    • Real-world examples of applying the scientific method to marketing
    • Establishing goals and metrics for digital marketing

Module 2: Building and Testing Hypotheses in Marketing

  • Lesson 2.1: What Makes a Good Hypothesis?
    • Formulating testable, specific, and measurable hypotheses
    • Types of hypotheses in marketing: ad performance, audience segmentation, message relevance
  • Lesson 2.2: Designing Marketing Experiments
    • Experiment design basics: control groups, sample sizes, and variable isolation
    • Different types of marketing experiments: A/B testing, multivariate testing, and sequential testing
    • Common mistakes to avoid (sample bias, overlapping audiences, seasonality)

Module 3: A/B Testing Foundations

  • Lesson 3.1: Introduction to A/B Testing in Digital Marketing
    • A/B testing principles and when to use them
    • Understanding statistical significance and power in A/B testing
  • Lesson 3.2: Tools and Platforms for A/B Testing
    • Overview of testing platforms: Google Optimize, Optimizely, VWO, and native ad testing tools (Facebook, Google Ads)
    • Setting up your first A/B test: example walkthrough

Module 4: Applying A/B Testing Across Digital Marketing Channels

  • Lesson 4.1: A/B Testing in Email Marketing
    • Hypothesis examples for subject lines, content layout, personalization, and CTAs
    • Interpreting results and optimizing based on data
  • Lesson 4.2: A/B Testing in Social Media Marketing
    • Testing audience segments, ad creatives, and messaging
    • Analyzing social media ad tests: CTR, engagement, and conversion rates
  • Lesson 4.3: A/B Testing in PPC and Display Advertising
    • Testing ad copy, keywords, bidding strategies, and landing pages
    • Calculating ROI and optimizing based on A/B test outcomes
  • Lesson 4.4: A/B Testing for Website and Landing Page Optimization
    • Testing design elements, messaging, and user flows
    • Identifying metrics (e.g., bounce rate, time on page, conversions) for website testing
