Data-Driven Digital Marketing: Applying the Scientific Method

A comprehensive course for mastering data-driven marketing through the scientific method and A/B testing.

Course Benefits

Eliminate Guesswork

Learn how to use data to drive marketing decisions, reducing uncertainty and increasing effectiveness.

Hands-On Experience

Gain practical experience with real-world case studies and a capstone project that applies your learning.

Optimize ROI

Use A/B testing and statistical analysis to maximize return on investment for all marketing channels.

Course Curriculum

Module 1: Introduction to Data-Driven Marketing and the Scientific Method

  • Lesson 1.1: Overview of Data-Driven Marketing - Why data matters in decision-making.
  • Lesson 1.2: The Scientific Method in Marketing - Structuring marketing as an experiment.

Module 2: Building and Testing Hypotheses in Marketing

  • Lesson 2.1: What Makes a Good Hypothesis? - Creating measurable and relevant hypotheses.
  • Lesson 2.2: Designing Marketing Experiments - Steps to create testable experiments.

Module 3: A/B Testing Foundations

  • Lesson 3.1: Introduction to A/B Testing in Digital Marketing - Basics and benefits of A/B testing.
  • Lesson 3.2: Tools and Platforms for A/B Testing - Reviewing software options for testing.

Module 4: Applying A/B Testing Across Digital Marketing Channels

  • Lesson 4.1: A/B Testing in Email Marketing - Test email campaigns for better performance.
  • Lesson 4.2: A/B Testing in Social Media Marketing - Optimize social ads and posts.
  • Lesson 4.3: A/B Testing in PPC and Display Advertising - Improve ad performance.
  • Lesson 4.4: A/B Testing for Website and Landing Page Optimization - Increase conversions with website tests.

Module 5: Advanced Testing Techniques

  • Lesson 5.1: Multivariate Testing - Testing multiple variables simultaneously.
  • Lesson 5.2: Sequential Testing and Multi-Arm Bandit Testing - Methods to improve test efficiency.
  • Lesson 5.3: Experimenting with Personalization - Test personalized marketing approaches.

Module 6: Data Analysis and Interpretation

  • Lesson 6.1: Analyzing Test Results with Statistical Rigor - Avoiding bias in data interpretation.
  • Lesson 6.2: Drawing Insights and Making Data-Driven Decisions - Making actionable marketing decisions.

Module 7: Scaling Marketing Experiments

  • Lesson 7.1: Creating a Culture of Experimentation - Encouraging continuous testing in teams.
  • Lesson 7.2: Scaling Successful Tests - Expanding successful experiments organization-wide.

Module 8: Practical Applications and Capstone Project

  • Lesson 8.1: Case Studies in Data-Driven Marketing Success - Learning from real-world examples.
  • Lesson 8.2: Capstone Project: Designing and Running a Full Marketing Experiment - Apply your knowledge in a hands-on project.
